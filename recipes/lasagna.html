Lasagna

Goated ahhhh lasagna with a fire bechamel inbetween the layers

bolognese preperation

  • 500 grams beef & pork minced
  • 1x can of tomatoes
  • tomato paste
  • equal parts carrot, red onion and celery
  • salt

    • Bechamel preperation

  • milk
  • salt
  • nutmeg
  • white pepper
  • flour
  • butter

    • Steps

    Ragu prep

    combine the carrots, red onion and celery into a pot and salt them to begin sweating them down

    in a seperate pan on a high heat add some olive oil to the pan and coat the pan and put down the meat into the pan letting it get a myard reaction so it gets nice and cripsy before moving it to cook through fully

    once meat is cooked through add to the large pot with the vegetables and add tomato paste *please be sure to cook down the tomato paste so it is not raw

    after tomato paste is cooked down and incorporated into the meat and vegetable medley add some tomato sauce 3 ladles full and let it cook on a simmer for 4 hours

    Bechamel prep

    create roux with flour and butter

    bring to heat milk seasoned with salt, nutmeg and white pepper

    combine the flour and butter roux with the milk and whisk until thickened

    put aside in a seperate dish once desired thickness is attained and cover with a plastic film to not have a film develop on the top of the bechamel

    Lasagna sheets prep

    can make by hand or buy store bought sheets doesnt matter

    blanche sheets in boiling salted water for 30 seconds then dunk them into a cold pot of water to cook them so they are ready to be baked

    put olive oil between the sheets so they do not stick to one another

    Lasagna Assembly

    layer sheets > bechamel > ragu > parmesan cheese *use 36 month ages parmesan > repeat until desired height is reached / all resources exhausted

    at the higher levels of the lasanga you can thin down the bechamel with a little bit of milk to make it softer

    Baking

    put in oven at 200 degrees celsius for 30 minutes

    let it sit for 30 minutes before cutting
