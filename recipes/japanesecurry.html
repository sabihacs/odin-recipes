Japense Curry w/ Fried Chicken Cutlet

low effort easy japanese curry with rice and chicken cutlets

ingredients

  • chicken thigh fillets
  • japense curry roux
  • potatoes
  • onion
  • chicken stock cube
  • short grain rice
  • panko bread crumbs
  • flour
  • eggs

    • Curry Prep

    cube the potatoes and thinly slice the onions add to pan with oil and boil water in a kettle for the stock cube

    add boiling water and the stock cube then add the curry roux and mix attentively, turn the pan to a simmer

    stir until desired thickness of the curry is reached

    Katsu prep

    in 3 seperate bowls add flour, breadcrumbs and eggwash for the breading

    flatten chicken thigh fillets and bread them in this order > flour > egg wash > breadcrumbs

    bring oil to heat and cook the crumbed fillets for 3 minutes per side

    Rice prep

    measure out desired amount of rice and wash it and add 1.5x amount of water to the rice to the rice cooker

    cook in rice cooker and let it go beast mode

    assembly

    add rice to plate pour some curry over it and add katsu over the rice *can slice the katsu into even slices for easier eating experience
